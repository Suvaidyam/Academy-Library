H.E. Mr. Nara Chandrababu Naidu

Chief Minister, Andhra Pradesh

Back

H.E. Chandrababu Naidu is a visionary leader and a transformative figure in Indian politics, with over four decades of public service. Renowned for pioneering reforms in technology, infrastructure, and governance, he has positioned Andhra Pradesh as a global investment hub. As Chief Minister, Mr. Naidu integrated technology into governance, streamlined decision-making, and advanced public service delivery. His initiatives in education have made the state a centre for human capital development, producing over 100,000 engineers annually.

A champion of rural development, he empowered women and alleviated poverty through self-help groups like DWCRA. His focus on inclusive growth, health, and employment has significantly uplifted farmers and marginalized communities. Globally acknowledged for his contributions, Mr. Naidu has received accolades like IT Indian of the Millennium and Business Person of the Year (The Economic Times). Oracle’s Profit magazine recognized him as one of the “hidden seven” leaders transforming the world through innovation.
