About IGGAARL

About

Established in 2022, the Indo-German Global Academy for Agroecology Research and Learning (IGGAARL) is a collaborative initiative between the Government of Andhra Pradesh and the Government of Germany to advance agroecology research and learning. IGGAARL serves as a global hub for knowledge exchange, capacity building, and innovation in agroecology, with a focus on sustainable farming practices and climate resilience. IGGAARL is dedicated to advancing agroecological approaches that strengthen biodiversity conservation and climate resilience. By fostering collaboration among farmers, researchers, and policymakers, IGGAARL seeks to enable sustainable transformation in agriculture and support sustainable livelihoods.

Vision

IGGAARL is envisioned to evolve as a global learning and knowledge hub for agroecology and Natural Farming.

Aim & Focus Areas

The aim of IGGAARL is to establish itself as an economically self-reliant global centre of excellence, advancing a farmer-centric approach to agroecology, agriculture-based livelihoods, value chains, and related domains within Natural Farming systems.

IGGAARL is being developed as a lighthouse centre and knowledge hub for agroecology, generating scientific evidence and promoting both traditional knowledge and innovative agroecological and Natural Farming practices. Its key focus areas include:

  1. Research, Development and Innovation
  2. Academics, Learning and Education
  3. Knowledge Management and Upscaling
