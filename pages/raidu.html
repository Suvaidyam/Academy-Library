Mr. D. V. Raidu, IAS (Retd.)

Advisor, Rythu Sadhikara Samstha

Back

D. V. Raidu is a senior official with expertise in soil science and extensive experience in agriculture. With 17 years dedicated to promoting Community-Managed Natural Farming, he focuses on empowering women-led CBOs to create sustainable, agroecological livelihoods and tackle climate challenges.

A practising natural farmer in Telangana, he cultivates an 8-acre food forest with crops like oil palm, guava, and vegetables, aiming for certification and carbon credits. Currently, he advises on natural farming science for the Rythu Sadhikara Samstha (RySS) under the APCNF project.
