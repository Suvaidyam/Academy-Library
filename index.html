    Welcome to IGGAARL - Indo-German Global Academy for Agroecology Research and Learning

    Indo-German Global Academy for Agroecology Research and Learning (IGGAARL) is a collaborative initiative between the Government of Andhra Pradesh and the Government of Germany, aimed at promoting agroecological research and learning. The academy serves as a global hub for knowledge exchange, capacity building, and innovation in agroecology, with a focus on sustainable agricultural practices and climate resilience.

    Academics

    Academy is offering three programs, Under Graduation, Post Graduation, and Certificate. Each programs is tailored to meet the need of the learner. Click on the below programs to know what courses are being offered under each program.

    Graduation

    Graduation program has experiential learning courses. Courses are conducted through classroom sessions, field classes, online classes, and field practice with much emphasis on learning by doing. Field-based learnings are evaluated at regular intervals.

    Post Graduation

    Post-graduation program has courses that are specifically targeted to enhance the learnings of graduates from agroecology background. These experiential learning courses are conducted through classroom sessions, field classes, online classes, and own field learning.

    Certificate

    Certificate program has courses of shorter duration with hybrid learning approach that are either self-paced or scheduled.

    About Research

    Research Collaborations

    The Academy’s Research is fundamentally aimed at generating robust, science-based evidence on natural farming and agroecology practices, with a strong emphasis on sustainability, farmer empowerment, and environmental resilience. This mission is executed through a comprehensive and collaborative approach that integrates multiple research categories and engages a diverse group of stakeholders.

    Our Academy researchers, alongside about one thousand farmer scientists, farmer mentors, and interns, are actively engaged in diverse research activities reflecting our commitment to participatory research and empowerment of the farmers.
