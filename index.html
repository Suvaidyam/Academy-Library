    Welcome to IGGAARL - Indo-German Global Academy for Agroecology Research and Learning

    IGGAARL was established in 2022 in Pulivendula, Y.S.R. Kadapa, Andhra Pradesh. There are 19 district-level learning centres across Andhra Pradesh apart from IGGARL. These 19 sub-centres actively conduct learning activities to enhance agroecological education and training and research activity ensuring widespread impact and accessibility.

    Admissions for Natural Forming

    The Natural Forming program focuses on the study and application of natural material formation processes, including geological, metallurgical, and organic transformations. Students gain hands-on experience with natural shaping techniques, sustainable material use, and scientific analysis of natural forms. This program blends theory with practical learning to prepare individuals for careers in environmental science, materials engineering, or artisan crafts.

    • Focus on eco-friendly and sustainable forming processes.
    • Hands-on training in natural material manipulation.
    • Study of geological and environmental formation systems.
    • Interdisciplinary approach combining science, art, and engineering.
    • Opportunities for fieldwork and research projects.
    • Ideal for careers in materials science, geology, environmental design, or crafts.
